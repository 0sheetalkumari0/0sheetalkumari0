<div align="center">

### `⟨ who_i_am.init() ⟩`

</div>

<br>

> *"I don't just write code — I build feelings."*

I'm someone who fell in love with the space where **creativity and logic collide**. Every project I touch starts the same way: a blank canvas, a quiet mind, and a burning question — *what if this could be beautiful?*

I'm driven by **curiosity** — the kind that makes you pull apart how things work just to understand them better, then put them back together in a way that feels more alive. I obsess over **clean interfaces**, the kind where everything feels inevitable, where the user never has to think. That gap between friction and flow? That's where I live.

**Problem-solving** isn't just a skill to me — it's almost meditative. There's something deeply satisfying about staring at a broken system and finding the exact thread that makes it whole again.

On most days you'll find me:
- Chasing that perfect design decision for 3 hours 🎯
- Reading about technology I don't fully understand yet 📡
- Sketching UI ideas in the margins of everything 🎨
- Wondering why the beautiful solution is always the simple one ✦

I believe technology should feel **human**. Warm. Intuitive. Like it was made by someone who actually cares — because it was.
